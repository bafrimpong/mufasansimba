Mufasa & Scar

the variant

This game is a variant of the Lion King where I decided to create a totally new concept out of the existing! What I am trying to achieve here is that instead of the usual enemity between Simba & Mufasa where they struggle for the kingship, they will play a game in order to determine winner.

Between them, anyone who gets the highest score or points after the game will be the person to climb on the thrown as the King

How to Play

Here are a few points to keep in mind when playing the game to become the King as Simba or Mufasa

  • At a point in time, a player can only move three (3) steps or spaces either horizontally or vertically and not more moves
  • Simba will start the game with the view that Mufasa knows how to win over Simba!
  • Around the kingdom (animal kingdom land) a player will find boxes of magic which when collected, will make you strong. The more you pick the stronger you become
  • Simba and the Mufasa will engage in a fearce fight but due to your training you can attack and defend all the time
